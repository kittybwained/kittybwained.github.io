By default, the P/390 MVS/ESA 5.2.2 ADCD has the JES2 "job ended" message disabled.
If you wish to reenable this, Edit SYS1.PARMLIB(MPFLST00) and remove the line beginning with $HASP395. You can also enable any other supressed messages here.

Credit goes to racingmars in the Moshix Discord server for figuring out how to fix this, I'm just writing it down here so I can remember it.
